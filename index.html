About WeArt

WeArt is dedicated to preserving and promoting the rich tradition of Khmer painting and drawing. Our mission is to document traditional techniques, support contemporary artists working in traditional styles, and educate the public about this important aspect of Cambodian cultural heritage.

We work with master artists, cultural organizations, and educational institutions to ensure that Khmer painting traditions continue to thrive and evolve.
